VISION & MISSION

VISION:

The Vision of Dormaa West District Assembly is to facilitate the improvement in living standard and quality of life of the people and also facilitate the provision of an enabling environment for good governance and the overall development of the District.

MISSION:

The Dormaa West District Assembly exist to facilitate the improvement to the standard of living of the people ,through effective formulation and implementation of policies ,projects and programmes which will result in the achievement of socio-economic development and the creation of an enabling environment for private sector development.
